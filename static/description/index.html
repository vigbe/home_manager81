Real Estate Property Management Features

Dashboard
Property Stats
  • No. of Properties (Total, Available, Sold, Leased, On Sale, & On Lease)
Rent Stats
  • Draft Contracts, Running Contracts, Expired Contracts, Total Rent Collections, & Invoice Due Reminders
Sale Stats
  • Total No. of Properties (Booked & Sold), & Total Selling Invoice Amounts
Graphs on Dashboard
  • Property Types, Property Status, Broker wise Rented Properties, Property Rent Invoice Due vs Paid, & Property Sold Invoice Due vs Paid
Property Management (Single & Multiple)
Main Property & Sub Properties
  • Multi Property functionality if you have more than one property at same location.
Types
  • Manage all types of properties: Residential, Commercial, Industrial, & Land
Area Measurements
  • Allow to add different area of properties with calculations. (Bedroom, Bathroom, Kitchen, Hall, Backyard, Parking, & Etc.)
Images, Documents, & Floor Plans
  • Allow to add Property Images, Documents, Floor Plans
Amenities, Features, & Nearby connectivity
  • Allow to add or create Property wise different Amenities, Features, Nearby connectivity
Brochures
  • PDF Report as a brochures of property with complete details
Open Property in Google Map Geo Location
  • Open property directly on Google map based on Geo Location coordinates (NO REQUIREMENT OF GOOGLE MAP API KEY)
Certificates & Tags
  • Mark property with different tags, Able to manage different safety related certificates
Rent, Sale, Booking, Available, Draft Status
  • Manage property status wise
Views
  • Kanban, Tree, Form, Pivot, & Activity
Search Panel, Filters & Group By
  • Search Panel (Property Type wise, Property Status wise, & Rent/Sale wise), Group By (Landlord, Country, Status, & Main Property)
Property Booking & Leads Management
Leads Management
  • Able to create leads property wise with Odoo CRM
  • Smart Action button to view all leads from Property
Property Booking
  • Book property through CRM for Sale & Lease
  • Token Amount based booking for Property Sell
  • Refund Amount functionality for Property Booking Customers
  • Customer Email Notification when property booked.
  • Activity Management for followup customers
Property Sales Management
Advance Booking with Deposit
  • Advance book facility with Deposit Amount
  • Refund facility for cancelled Users
Property Selling Contract
  • Selling Contract with full details PDF Reports
Property Selling Invoice Management with Full Payment & Installments
  • Options of Payment: Full Payment, Installment (Monthly, Quarterly, & Yearly)
Brokerage/Commission Feature for Third Party Sales
  • Brokerage/Commission based on fixed price & percentage of total amount
Email Notification for Sales Contract
  • Sales contract full details with PDF Reports in E-mail
Property Rent or Lease Management
Rent/Lease with different durations (Days, Months, & Years)
  • Options of rent/lease contract create for: Days, Months, & Years
Payment Terms
  • Payment Terms while create contract: Daily, Monthly, Quarterly, & Yearly
Property Rent Invoice Management with Full Payment & Installments
  • Options of Payment: Full Payment, Installments
In Installment Features of AUTO INVOICE CREATE & REMINDER
  • Fully automated invoice creation in-case missed creating rent invoice on time
Contract Renew Features
  • Options for contract renew or extend from existing contract
Early Auto Invoice Create for Next Month Invoice
  • You can set how many days before of next month you want to create auto invoice.
Ability to Integrate ONLINE PAYMENT (No Customization)
  • Ability to Integrate ONLINE PAYMENT without any customization with Payment Gateways (Stripe, Paypal, Alipay, Mollie, Authorize.net, & Adyen)
Email Notification for Rent Invoice Reminder
  • Auto Email sent with Invoice for upcoming month rent invoice.
Broker/Agent Management
Commission (Fixed & Percentage)
  • Options of commission fixed or percentage
Commission Type (Only onetime or Each Month) of Invoice
  • Options of adding commission either only for first month or each month in invoice
Commission History
  • Commission History in Property & in agent record
Odoo Bill for Commission
  • Options of create Odoo Invoicing bill for commission
Agent/Broker Menu
  • Separate management menu of Broker/Agent
Invoice Management (Installment & Full)
Standard Odoo Invoicing Integration
  • For Invoice management standard Odoo Invoicing app integration
Accounting Integration
  • With Odoo Invoice/Bill easily able manage Accounting
Maintenance Handling
Standard Odoo Maintenance App Integration
  • Options of create maintenance of property
Team/Speciality wise Maintenance
  • Options of assign request to different teams
Invoice for Maintenance Work
  • Options of create invoice for maintenance works
Employees/Maintenance Users Work Hours Logging
  • Able to log work hours of maintenance
Email Notification (Auto Reminders)
Rent/Sale Contract Notification
  • Rent & Sale contract email notification with attachment
Rent/Sale Invoice Reminder Notification
  • Invoice reminder for rent/sale invoices
Property Booking Notification
  • Property Booking notification
Reports (Sale Wise, Rent Wise, & Property Wise)
XLS Reports
  • Rent/Sale reports between two dates.
  • Landlord wise reports for property sold/rent.
PDF Reports
  • Property Brochures
  • Property Booking
  • Rent Contract
  • Sale Contract
Property Extra Services, Deposit, & Penalty
Extra Services
  • Options of Extra service add while create new rent/lease contract
Deposit
  • Options of deposit while property booking
Penalty
  • Options of create invoice with specific reason while contract running for rent/lease
